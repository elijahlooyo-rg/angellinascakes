Our Services

From custom creations to delivery, we offer a range of services to make your celebrations unforgettable. Explore what we can do for you!

Custom Cake Design

Bring your vision to life with our bespoke cake designs. Whether it's a wedding, birthday, or special event, our expert bakers create edible masterpieces tailored to your theme and preferences.

Delivery Service

We deliver fresh cakes right to your doorstep. Our delivery service ensures your treats arrive in perfect condition, with careful packaging and timely arrival.

Catering for Events

Planning a party? Let us handle the desserts! We provide catering services for weddings, corporate events, birthdays, and more, with a variety of cake options to suit your guests.

Advance Orders

Place your orders in advance to ensure availability for your special occasions. We recommend booking custom cakes at least 2 weeks ahead.

Dietary Options

We cater to various dietary needs, including gluten-free, vegan, and sugar-free options. Let us know your requirements, and we'll create delicious alternatives.

In-Store Pickup

Prefer to pick up your order? Visit our store in Ndeeba, Kampala, for a personal touch. Browse our display cakes and get expert advice from our team.
